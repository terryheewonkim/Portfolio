Description

This is a capstone project my group is currently working on in conjunction with Purdue Conferences, our sponsor. Our goal is to create an MVP of a ticketing website for Purdue Conferences so that event attendees can purchase and receive their tickets easily.

We are developing the attendee side of the website more so than the organizer side, so our work will reflect both the front end and back end development.
